Issue: Private Gated Community Management

current access and service management system in the gated community is manual and presents several issues:

  • access control: Manual registration at the security booth causes long lines, data errors, and low traceability.
  • Dispersed service management: Payments, service requests, and reports are handled through different channels (phone calls, emails, in-person), making tracking and transparency difficult.
  • Lack of reservation visibility: There is no accessible system to reserve common areas, leading to confusion about their availability.
  • No financial transparency: Residents cannot consult income, expenses, or how the community budget is being used.
