Issue: Private Gated Community Management

Current access and service management system un the gated community is manual and presents several issues:

  • Access control: Manual registration at the security booth causes long lines, data errors and low traceability.
  • Dispersed service managementL Payments, servicce requests, and reports are handled through different channels (phone calls, emails, in-person), making tracking and transperncy difficult.
  • Lack of reservation visivility: There is no accessible system to reserve common areas, leadong to confusion about their availability.
  • No financial transparency: Residents cannot consult income, expenses, or how the community budget is being used.
